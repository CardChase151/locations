# CardChase Partner Tiers - Pricing & Features

> **DRAFT** - Subject to change

---

## Tier Breakdown

### FREE ($0/mo)
**What Shop Gets:**
- Listed in app (name, address only)
- Shows in "Nearby" filter only
- Not clickable - no profile page

**What Users See:**
- Shop name and distance in list
- Cannot tap for details
- No photos, no events

**Why Upgrade?** "I want people to actually click on my shop"

---

### BASIC ($50/mo)
**What Shop Gets:**
- Clickable profile with dedicated page
- 1 shop photo
- 2 event nights per month
- 3 photos per event
- RSVP tracking on events
- Award eligibility - compete for CardChase awards
- No analytics
- No search boost

**What Users See:**
- Tap to see shop profile
- Shop photo, hours, description
- Events with RSVP button

**Why Upgrade?** "I want to see my stats and stand out in search"

---

### ENHANCED ($150/mo)
**What Shop Gets:**
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- **Verified badge** (green checkmark)
- 5 shop photos
- 10 photos per event
- **Search boost within 10mi**
- **Full analytics dashboard:**
  - Profile views
  - Clicks
  - Followers
  - Trade meetup counter
  - Where your traders are coming from
- **Notify followers** (1x per week)
- Award eligibility - compete for CardChase awards

**What Users See:**
- Green "Verified" badge
- More photos
- Events in home feed
- Push notification when shop posts (1x/week)

**Why Upgrade?** "I want to dominate my area and reach beyond my followers"

---

### PREMIUM ($300/mo)
**What Shop Gets:**
- Everything in Enhanced, plus:
- **Premium badge** (gold crown)
- **Featured tab placement** (default view for users)
- Unlimited shop photos
- Unlimited event photos
- 4 event nights per month
- **Search boost within 30mi**
- **Unlimited follower notifications**
- **State-wide event promotion:**
  - Publish event (even months ahead)
  - All users in state notified 1x/month
  - Final 4 weeks: notified 1x/week
  - Stops if user RSVPs or Declines
- **Loyalty program** - exclusive perks and rewards
- Monthly ROI report
- Award eligibility with priority consideration
- Physical "CardChase Premium Partner" decal

**What Users See:**
- Gold "Premium" badge with glow
- Shop in Featured tab (default landing)
- Rich profile with unlimited photos
- State-wide event notifications (can RSVP or Decline to stop)

## Notification System

| Tier | Who Gets Notified | Frequency |
|------|-------------------|-----------|
| Free | Nobody | - |
| Basic | Nobody | - |
| Enhanced | Followers only | 1x per week |
| Premium | Followers | Unlimited |
| Premium | State-wide (events) | See below |

### Premium State-Wide Event Notifications
```
Event published -> Users in state notified

Timeline:
- 4+ weeks out: 1x per month
- 4 weeks out: 1x per week
- 3 weeks out: 1x per week
- 2 weeks out: 1x per week
- 1 week out: 1x per week
- Event day!

User actions that STOP notifications:
- RSVP'd -> No more reminders (they're going)
- Declined -> No more reminders (they said no)
```

## The Value Pitch

**Free -> Basic:** "Get a real presence. Let traders click your profile, see your shop, and RSVP to your events."

**Basic -> Enhanced:** "See your stats - views, followers, trades, where they're coming from. Get verified, boost your search ranking, and notify your followers weekly."

**Enhanced -> Premium:** "Own your state. Featured placement, unlimited content, state-wide event promotion, loyalty rewards. Be THE Pokemon destination."
